    I’ve always been drawn to technology — the kind of kid who spent hours glued to a computer, exploring every corner of the internet and wondering what else was possible.     Once I realized I could build things from scratch, I was hooked. I already had the imagination and drive — now I had the tools. I see endless possibilities in tech, and I’m eager to explore all of them.
